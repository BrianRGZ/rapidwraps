Frequently asked questions

Commonly asked things

What is a wrap?

A vehicle wrap is the better alternative to a paint job. A vinyl wrap will both style and protect your vehicle at the same time.

Wheter it is to simply change the appearance, or to stand apart from the crowd, Rapid Wraps will provide the best solution.

How much?

Because every vehicle and vinyl preferences are different, we encourage you to contact us for an immediate quote.

A quality vinyl wrap appreciates the value of the vehicle, because you're ensuring paint protection, and preserving the original color.

Damage my car?

Absolutely NOT, because we use only the best materials with the best adhesives properties.

The vinyl wrap is strong enough to hold itself in place for years. Yet, easy enough to remove by hand if wanting to.

Vinyl wrap life?

Vinyl manufacturers guarantee a film life from 5-7 years.

However, other factors such as appropriate car care and different climates may affect the longevity.

Note: We offer 1 year installer warranty.
